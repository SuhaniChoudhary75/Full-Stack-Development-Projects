Find detailed information about various modes of public transport in Pune.

Bus

Explore bus routes, timings, and fares.Types of Buses: Regular Buses: These are standard city buses that operate on various routes throughout Pune. Midi Buses: Smaller buses designed to navigate narrow roads and less crowded areas. AC Buses: Air-conditioned buses that offer a more comfortable ride, usually at a higher fare. BRTS Buses: These operate on the Bus Rapid Transit System (BRTS) corridors, providing faster transit with dedicated lanes. Route Network: PMPML operates over 350 routes covering various parts of Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad. This network connects residential areas, business hubs, educational institutions, and major landmarks. Fares: The fare structure is based on the distance traveled. It's relatively affordable, with different rates for regular and AC buses. PMPML also offers various passes for daily, weekly, and monthly travel, which are cost-effective for frequent travelers.

Metro

Get details on metro lines, schedules, and ticketing.Service Provider: Pune Metro is managed by the Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Limited (Maha-Metro), a joint venture between the Government of India and the Government of Maharashtra. Operational Lines: Pune Metro currently has two major lines in operation, with plans for further expansion: Purple Line: Covering the east-west corridor from PCMC (Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation) to Swargate. Aqua Line: Running from Vanaz to Ramwadi in the north-south corridor.

Railway

Find out about railway stations, routes, and tickets.Connectivity: Pune Junction connects to several major cities across India, including Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Chennai. It is well-integrated into the national railway network. Platforms: Pune Junction has multiple platforms serving a range of long-distance and local trains. Facilities at the station include waiting rooms, ticket counters, food stalls, and restrooms.Long-Distance Trains: Some notable trains operating from Pune Junction include: Deccan Queen Express: Connects Pune with Mumbai. Nandigram Express: Connects Pune with Delhi. Koyna Express: Runs between Pune and Mumbai. Udyan Express: Connects Pune with Bengaluru. Sahyadri Express: Runs between Pune and Mumbai. Local Trains: Pune also has suburban train services connecting it with nearby cities and towns, including local trains running to and from Mumbai.

Flight

Information on Flight fares and routes.Terminals: Domestic Terminal: Handles flights within India. It features amenities such as check-in counters, baggage claim, lounges, food and beverage outlets, and retail shops. International Terminal: Services international flights, with facilities for customs, immigration, duty-free shopping, and international food options. Runways: Pune Airport has a single runway that accommodates both domestic and international flights. Domestic Airlines: Major airlines operating at Pune Airport include Air India, IndiGo, SpiceJet, GoAir, and Vistara. Key domestic destinations include Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata. International Airlines: Airlines such as Air India, Emirates, Qatar Airways, and SriLankan Airlines operate international flights. International destinations include Dubai, Doha, Singapore, Colombo, and London.
